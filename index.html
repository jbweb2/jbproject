Useful Links

Australia Computer Society

The Australian Computer Society (ACS) is an association for information and communications technology professionals in Australia. They have over 22,000 members Australia-wide. It's objective is to "to advance professional excellence in information technology" and "to promote the development of Australian information and communications technology resources".
